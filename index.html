Colombia

A good travel guide

Colombia is a country located in South America that has many wonderful places to go such as its Capital Bogota. Colombia is known for its very rich culture and its traditions such as la Feria de las Flores, also for having good food such as la Bandeja Paisa, and arroz con leche. Colombia also has very nice tourist attractions such as Quimbaya, Salento, and Santa Rosa.

Colombia had gained their independence on July 20th of 1810 after Spain had controlled Colombia and they were able to fight back. The three colors on the Colombia Flag are Yellow, Blue, and Red these colors can represent Gold in the country, the two oceans surrounding Colombia, and the blood spilled to gain their independence.
